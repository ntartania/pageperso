Research

My main topics of research are related to the engineering of social machines: multi-agent systems where agents are mostly people. Social machines live mostly on the Web, but I’m very interested in decentralization, so I’m also very interested in alternative architectures, including peer-to-peer systems and ad-hoc networks.
